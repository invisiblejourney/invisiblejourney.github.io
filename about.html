This is a science fiction series about time, space, and life.

  • It is not another science fiction series that are full of travelling back to many years ago stories.
  • It is not another science fiction series in which jumping between galaxies is faster than jumping out your car.
  • It is not another science fiction series full of dogfight between spaceships.
  • It is not another science fiction series that telling the stories happened in a space setup.

It is different, in a good way.
